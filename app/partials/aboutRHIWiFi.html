Why did we start this work? Our communities digital infrastucture, Hurricane Sandy, lack of access, communication and to provide resources such as housing, education, legal aid, employment and community events . . .

Providing free wireless internet connection for the cummunity as a community. Partbering with local business and organizations to help create our mesh network. A mesh network is wireless ntwork which works through nodes which are like little antennas that send out and recieve data. A mesh network is a good because its a decentralizied system so if one of the nodes are damaged the system will still be up and running. . . . .
